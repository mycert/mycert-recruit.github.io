Mobile Threat Analyst 
MyCERT is looking for Mobile Threat Analyst. The job descriptions are as follows:
1.  Collect and analyse mobile binaries for malicious activities or vulnerabilities.
2.  Develop security tools related to information security research.
3.  To develop technical training module and conduct training /
    workshop to the general public and / or relevant authorities.
4.  Produce periodical report and / or analysis on the incidents reported.
5.  Produce alerts and advisories related to mobile incident.

Education Requirement   :   Degree
Area of Study/ Major    :   Computer Science / Information Technology / Network Security
Certification           :   Not required, but having SANS related cert is an add-on!
Experience Requirement  :   1 years relevant experience in Programming (Highly recommended)/
                            Mobile Security/ Computer Security
                            Fresh graduate are encouraged to apply too!
			    
*All applicants must be Malaysian Citizens.
**There will be background screening before interview process.		    
			    
Specific Qualities
(if any)                :   Knowledge of Mobile Technology and Mobile Operating System.
                        :   Familiar with OWASP Top Ten and OWASP Mobile Top Ten.
                        :   Detailed knowledge of Network based attacks and their mitigation.
                        :   Knowledge of Network related protocols i.e. TCP/IP.
                        :   Knowledge of Scripting using any programming language 
                            i.e Python, PHP, Java, etc...
                        :   Knowledge of system security vulnerabilities and remediation techniques.
                        :   Ability to produce Technical Reports.
 
Please sent your CV (not resume) to lebahnet@cybersecurity.my with subject "Mobile Threat Analyst application".
We look forward for your application, Thank You!
